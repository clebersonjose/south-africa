Recipe

2 cups Pea or Chana flour
Three quarter cup plain flour
2 tsp salt
1 tsp turmeric
2tsp masala (curry powder)
1 tbsp coriander
1 tsp chillie powder
2 tsp baking powder
3 green chillies
Chop fine 1 onion and a small tomato
3 - 4 Spinach leaves chop

Mix with water until it forms a batter
(Not too watery otherwise it will not come out round balls)
Deep fry in oil at medium heat.
